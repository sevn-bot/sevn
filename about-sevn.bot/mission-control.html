Mission Control is the owner dashboard served at /mission/ on your gateway. Use the preview below to explore each group:

  • Hover a tab for a one-line summary.
  • Tap a group (Core, Observability, …) to open that section.
  • Tap a tab inside a group to read what it does (preview only — unwired tabs may still show “coming soon” in the live UI).

On your own machine with dashboard.local_open, loopback access may skip login; tunneled or remote access requires the dashboard password.

This is a documentation preview, not your live gateway.
